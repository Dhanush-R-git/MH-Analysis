Welcome to MentaChat

MentaChat is an AI-powered chatbot designed to support mental health well-being. Our project aims to provide a safe and non-judgmental space for users to share their thoughts and receive supportive, helpful guidance.

Using cutting-edge NLP models, MentaChat analyzes user input, detects emotional states, and offers practical advice or coping strategies. Whether you're feeling anxious, stressed, or just need someone to talk to, MentaChat is here to help.
