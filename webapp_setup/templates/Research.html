Maṉa - A Comprehensive Mental Health Analysis & Research via Social Media Platforms

"Our project, Maṉa platforms are designed to analyze the mental health conditions through user interactions with social media. Maṉa research aims to transform raw social media data into meaningful insights that can help improve mental health care and inform broader social strategies".

Why Maṉa Research?

Real-Time Emotional Insights

It helps capture real-time public sentiment by analyzing users’ comments and expressions online, providing immediate insights into mental well-being.

Early Detection of Mental Health Issues

By accurately detecting negative emotions and subtle differences between moods, the research can identify early warning signs of mental distress.

Improved Policy and Market Research

The research supports public policy decisions and market research by revealing trends and patterns in how people express their feelings.

Bridging Gaps in Traditional Analysis

Traditional sentiment analysis often misses nuanced emotional cues due to informal language and cultural expressions. Maṉa research addresses these gaps with advanced natural language processing models.

Our Architecture

Our system leverages social media data to analyze mental health through sentiment analysis and AI-driven diagnostic models. We use a fine-tuned RoBERTa model for sentiment classification, and our dual approach includes a standard chatbot and a detailed assessment tool. The architecture comprises a data aggregation layer, a preprocessing module, advanced NLP modules, and secure storage for user data.
